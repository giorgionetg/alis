## FRUIT LOGISTICA 2026 — Quick Overview

**FRUIT LOGISTICA 2026** takes place in **Berlin (Messe Berlin) from 4–6 February 2026** and is a major international meeting point for the fresh produce industry. The event features **2,500+ exhibitors from 90+ countries** and attracts professionals from **150+ nations**.

The trade fair covers the full value chain and is structured around three core segments: **Fresh Produce**, **Machinery & Technology**, and **Logistics**. The 2026 theme is **“Let’s grow!”**, highlighting innovation, sustainability, and shared progress across the sector.

### Key highlights
- **FRUIT LOGISTICA Innovation Award (FLIA) — 20th edition:** award ceremony on **6 February 2026**.
- **New Product Showcases:** a format allowing exhibitors to present **up to two new products**.
- **Expert Forums:** a rich programme across **six stages** focusing on trends and solutions for the industry.
- **Organic Route:** a curated route featuring **200+ exhibitors** with certified organic products.
- **Fruitful Friday (6 February):** final-day programme featuring the **FLIA ceremony**, **FRUTIC Science Symposium** (with Leibniz ATB), and the **Mascot Race**.
